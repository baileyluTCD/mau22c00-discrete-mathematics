A [[graph]] traversal is considered to be _open_ if the [[vertex]] at which it starts is different to the [[vertex]] at which it ends.

> [!example]
> ![[Open Walk Image.png]]
> An _open_ [[Walk]] starting at $U$ and ending at $Z$.
> 
> $U \ne Z \therefore$ This is _open_.
