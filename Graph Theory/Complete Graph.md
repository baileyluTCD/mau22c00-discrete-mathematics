A _complete [[graph]]_ is a [[graph]] which has the **highest possible** number of [[Edge|edges]] connecting to each [[vertex]].

---
### Notation:
A _complete [[graph]]_ with $n$ [[Vertex|vertices]] is denoted as $K_n$
