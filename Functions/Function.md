A _function_ from a [[set]] $A$ to $B$ is a **rule** that assigns to each [[Object (Set Theory)|element]] of $A$ one [[Object (Set Theory)|element]] of $B$.

In this case $A$ is the [[Domain|domain]] and $B$ is the [[Codomain|codomain]].

---
### Example:
$A = \{1, 3, 7\}$
$B = \{1, 2, 5\}$
