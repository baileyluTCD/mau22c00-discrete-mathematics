A _monoid_ is any [[semigroup]] which has an [[identity element]] associated with it.

---
### Examples: Common Operations in $\mathbb R$

| [[Binary Operation]] | [[Semigroup]]         | [[Identity Element]] |
| -------------------- | --------------------- | -------------------- |
| Addition             | $(\mathbb R, +)$      | $0$                  |
| Multiplication       | $(\mathbb R, \times)$ | $1$                  |
