A [[Group]] is considered to be _Abelian_/[[Commutative]] if it's **associated** [[Binary Operation]] is [[Commutative]].

---
### Examples:

| [[Commutative]] [[Group]] | [[Invertible\|Inverted Element]] |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| $(R, +, 0)$               | $-x$                             |
| $Q* = Q/0, (Q*, x, 1)$    | $q^{-1} = \frac1q$               |
